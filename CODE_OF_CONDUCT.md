# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Keep technical discussion focused on the project.
- Protect other people's privacy and credentials.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, doxxing, publishing private data, deliberate disruption and sexualized conduct are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior inconsistent with this policy. Report security matters through private vulnerability reporting. Report other conduct concerns privately through the maintainer's GitHub profile.

This policy is based on the principles of the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
